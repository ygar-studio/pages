The Game

A riddle game with nothing to hide

Every question hides a single answer — a person, a place, a work of art. You uncover it one clue at a time. You start with two enigmatic riddles. No dates, no domain, no easy way in. Get it wrong, and a new clue is revealed. The further you go, the easier it gets — but the fewer points you score. The perfect round? Guessing with only the first two clues.

No account required. No ads. No interruptions. Just you, the clues, and the answer waiting to be found.

Features

Crafted with care

Progressive reveal
Six clues per question, ordered from cryptic riddle to near-giveaway. The fewer you need, the higher your score.
Three difficulty levels
Easy, Medium, Hard — each with its own curated question set within every category.
Smart autocomplete
Suggestions never reveal the category. No guessing by elimination — only real knowledge counts.
Statistics dashboard
Unlocked after 10 games: track your streak, solve rate, and average clues needed per answer.
Fully bilingual
Play in English or French. Every clue is crafted natively in both languages — no rough translations.
Cloud save
Progress syncs across devices via Google Play Games. Optional — the game works fully offline.
